Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a rude driver?

You can report an issue regarding your ride through the Customer Support found in the Report page of the app. We will take the issue seriously and will investigate the matter urgently. After a successful investigation, a formal report will be filed to the LTFRB to observe proper disciplinary actions to the affected parties.

The amount shown in your app is wrong!

We’re sorry for that! While we’re working on updating the fares for the different public utility vehicles, please let us know the incorrect fare by emailing us at contact@biyahe.com

Why isn't my booking accepted?

Sorry, booking a carpool ride for you may take some time! Ride-matching is subjected to the supply of cars under the carpool feature of the application.

Does Biyahe charge for a carpool?

No. The Carpool feature is absolutely free of charge.

My driver didn't show up!

Contact the driver via call or text message. The driver may be driving, so try again if s/he doesn’t pick up. If your driver is still unresponsive, cancel the booking on your app.

Why are there no directions outside Metro Manila?

We currently have no comprehensive data for areas outside Metro Manila, but don’t worry our team is working on it and will be available soon!

Why is the map not showing my exact location?

Make sure that the Location Services / GPS of your phone is enabled and Biyahe Application is allowed to use such.

Why is the map not updating real-time?

Make sure that your cellular data is turned on and have stable Internet connectivity.

Why can’t I find the place I’m looking for?

Sorry about that! Sometimes our search results don’t have certain location names but don’t worry, we’re working on it. For now, try searching by street address. We hope it works!

How can I cash-in?

You may choose between using a debit/credit card or using CLiQQ top-up that is available in 7-Eleven branches nationwide.

How can I reach you?

You may reach us through our support email contact@biyahe.com
