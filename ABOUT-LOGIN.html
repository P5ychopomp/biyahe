About Us

Biyahe is a navigational app designed to make public transportation commuting easier and more efficient for its users by integrating functional maps, carpooling system, and digital payment methods. Biyahe displays the best commuting route and mode of transportation possible on getting to your destination quickly and productively, yet cost-effective.
